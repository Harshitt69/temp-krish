About Us

about us

Located in the Western part of Paschim Medinipur, Jhargram is a paradise for nature lovers. The exotic forests of sal, teak, peasal and mahul along with a variety of birds, wild elephants, and deer in the vicinity make it a popular tourist destination. Adding to the charm of Jhargram, Krish Garden has become a favorite destination for tourists that want to closely experience nature. Located at a distance of 10 km from the station and close to NH6 Highway, it is easily accessible for tourists. Situated in the Salboni area of Jhargram, the amusement park is the perfect holiday destination for people of all ages, at a very affordable price. Covering almost 10 acres of area, Krish Garden has an amazement park as well as a spot for private parties or picnics. It also has an aquarium, a bird-watching point, a fish-feeding point, and a children’s play area. This place is a perfect venue for wedding celebrations, private parties, family get-togethers, music video shoots, pre-wedding and post-wedding photoshoots. The King’s Palace is a famous attraction close to Krish Garden. The Temple of Kanak Durga along the river Dulung is sure to enchant everyone with its old 435-year history. The beautiful architectural structure of the temple sculptured during the reign of the King of Chilkigarh is a visual treat for tourists from all over India. The mini zoo and zoological park are extremely attractive to tourists who enjoy discovering the unknown and unaffected beauty of nature. Be it a weekend getaway with family or a fun trip with friends, Krish Garden is sure to keep everyone entertained for at least a week.
